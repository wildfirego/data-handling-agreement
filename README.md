THIS AGREEMENT made as of the _ _ day of _ _ _ 20 _ , between SAMANVAY WEB PVT. LTD. (the "Employer"); and EMPLOYEE-NAME (the "Employee").  

1. DEFINITIONS  
1.1 THIRD PARTY means client, corporation, association, organization or entity, either public or private, or a person, including a public official, who is rendered services by the Employer, or is providing services to the Employer.  
1.2 RESOURCE is a digital or physical document file, anything that contains data or information. Resources could include documents, concept notes, discussion plans, screenshots, photographs, passwords, emails, messages or any articulation that contains information about the Third Party's work in language text, visual or auditory forms.  

2. IT IS AGREED AS FOLLOWS  
2.1 The Employee shall sometimes have access to Resources of the Third Party. The Employee shall not disclose any Resource, in whole or in part, or any copy or representation of it, to any person or entity outside of the Employer or entities defined explicitly by the Employer for that particular Resource. Every Resource might have a different legal binding as to who all it can be shared with. The Employee needs to clarify with the Employer if there is not enough clarity on the exact persons with whom such resources can be shared.  
2.2 The Employee agrees that at the termination of this contract, all Employer data and Third Party's data will be returned to the Employer and destroyed.  

3. BREACH OF CONTRACT  
3.1 This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Union of India, jurisdiction of state of Haryana.  
3.2 Legal action will be taken if the Employee breach's this DATA HANDLING AGREEMENT.
